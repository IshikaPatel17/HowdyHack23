WHAT IS HOWDY HEROS

We are a small website developed by four freshman engineering students. Together, we are the loudest, the proudest, members of the Fighting Texas Aggie class of 2027!

We created this website to help out our fellow Aggie students who need some academic support in their classes. Our site helps Aggie students get matched with Aggie tutors who fit their academic needs .

School's hard. Trust us, we are just as sleep deprived and stressed as y'all. So, use our site as a resource to find that support y'all need in order to succeed academically! Or, create an account as a tutor and help a fellow Aggie out and make some money.

HOW OUR SITE WORKS

For students:

It's simple: just sign up! Go to our sign up page and fill out some information like your name, contact information, and the class(es) you need help with, and we will connect you to one of our well qualified tutors instantly

For tutors:

Do you want to help some fellow Aggies while making some extra money? If so, create an account , it only takes a few minutes and you will get hired instantly! Just fill out some information about yourself, the class(es) you are proficent in, your rates, and the times you are available, and you are hired!
